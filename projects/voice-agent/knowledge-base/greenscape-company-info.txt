FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does a typical landscaping project take?
Small projects like flower bed installation take one to two days. Medium projects like a patio or garden redesign take three to five days. Large projects like full yard transformations can take two to four weeks.

Do you offer free estimates?
Yes, we offer free on-site estimates for all projects over five hundred dollars. The estimate visit usually takes thirty to forty-five minutes.

What about materials?
All materials are included in our quoted price. We use premium, locally sourced plants and materials whenever possible. We guarantee plant survival for one year with our maintenance plans.

Do you offer financing?
Yes, for projects over five thousand dollars we offer six and twelve month payment plans through a third-party financing partner. Zero percent interest for the first six months on approved credit.

What if it rains on my scheduled day?
We reschedule rain days at no extra charge. Your crew lead will contact you by seven a m if weather causes a delay.

Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. We hold a Florida Landscape Contractor License and carry two million dollars in general liability insurance. We are also bonded. All crew members are background-checked.

Do you handle HOA requirements?
Yes. If your HOA requires approval for landscaping changes, we can prepare the necessary documentation and plans for your submission.

What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept credit cards, debit cards, checks, ACH bank transfer, and Zelle. A fifty percent deposit is required for projects over two thousand dollars, with the balance due upon completion.
